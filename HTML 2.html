Introduction to Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world and a symbol of love. It is located in Agra, India and was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

History of Taj Mahal

The construction of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 and took around 20 years to complete. It is made of white marble and decorated with precious stones. The monument reflects Mughal architecture and attracts millions of tourists every year.

Architecture and Beauty

The Taj Mahal stands on the banks of the Yamuna River and is surrounded by beautiful gardens. Its symmetry, large dome, and detailed carvings make it a masterpiece of architecture. It looks different at different times of the day due to changing light.

Tourism Importance

The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Visitors from all over the world come to see its beauty and experience its historical significance.
